# 2026 Senate Election Turnout Estimates

**Methodology:**
- Based on 2026 Voting Eligible Population (VEP) estimates
- Midterm baseline: 45-50% turnout (vs. 63-64% in presidential years)
- Competitive races: +3-8 points above baseline
- Non-competitive races: baseline or lower
- State historical patterns considered

**Competitive Races:** Alaska, Georgia, Iowa, Maine, Michigan, Nebraska, New Hampshire, North Carolina, Ohio, Virginia

---
